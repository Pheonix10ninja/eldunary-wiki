The Crimson Convergence

History changed forever when the red star appeared over Eldunary for 40 days. [cite_start]It drew magic from the earth and put it into tangible forms like runes [cite: 296-297]. This event caused the rapid evolution of species; [cite_start]Humans became Folkwynd, and the light of the star killed every elf it touched, forcing survivors into the shadows where they became Darkmane Elves [cite: 298-302].
