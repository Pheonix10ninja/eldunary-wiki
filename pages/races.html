Bestiary & Races

Tharnex (The Insectoids)

Status: Critically Endangered (Only 1 known survivor: Vorryn)

Traits: Chitin armor, multiple eyes, echo-sense. They worship X'zel, the Hive Divinity of Memory.

History: Nearly extinct following the War of Fodon.

Goldhelms (Solari)

Height: 6ft+

Features: Golden eyes, ivory-blond hair.

Ability: Anti-magic; sole developers of Holo-tech.

Darkmane Elves (Naelvari)

Habitat: Dense forests of Grousea & Wrediff.

Features: Obsidian skin, bioluminescent eyes.

Combat: Guerrilla warfare, poison, and shadowstep.

Golems (Stoneborn)

Lifespan: Immortal (if undestroyed).

Origin: Created solely by the Golem King.

Magic: High affinity for Rune magic.

Ipadoras (Tidewalkers)

Features: Tentacle limbs, beaked mouths.

Magic: Water-bending and ink sigils.

Culture: Matriarchal clans led by Matrons.
