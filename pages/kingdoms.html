The Kingdoms

The geopolitical landscape of Criozevan in Year 1000 ACC[cite: 4].

Goldhelm Kingdom

Capital: Ovodon ("The Gilded Cradle")

Biome: Hilly, Light Forest

Trait: Technocratic Isolationists. Magic is banned; Holo-tech is used exclusively.

Kingdom of Fodon

Capital: Fodon

Biome: Arid Desert & Plains

History: Formerly divided into Bug, Magic, Death, and Fire districts before Lucifer (#6 Arashi) killed the leaders.

State of Akison

Capital: Akison

Biome: Jungle, Island

Cities: Kusamori, Oniofast, Neruvalis.

Trait: A trade hub connecting Criozevan to other continents.

Nikolem Kingdom

Capital: Suudon

Biome: Mountains, Swamp

Ruler: The Golem King.

Trait: Home to the Golems and huge underground systems.

Ipadora Kingdom

Capital: Ipadora Sanctuary

Biome: Underwater / Island

Trait: A sanctuary with little to no crime. The palace is submerged underwater.
